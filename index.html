Things we cannot take as bug or “Helpful Information”.

As of now, FiveM Crashes or Unable To Connect Errors (Depending on the error.) We cannot fix or help with something that is FiveM sided, if the error is stating something is wrong with our Server please submit a bug report and we will be on it ASAP!

Client Sided, anything that has to do with your PC / Game we cannot accept as a bug. This is due to the fact this has nothing to do with our community, but is an issue with your game / client sided Graphic Packs or Sound packs.

  • How Many Bugs Do I need to Find to get into the Dev Discord?
    A; As of now. You will need to find 3 Mid / High attention bugs.

  • What Perks / Access do I get if I gain access into the Dev Discord?
    A; Generally you will be able to help us testing Scripts, Cars, and more. We plan on adding more based on how many bugs you have found within the community.
